# 小学英语知识点 - 常用句型

## 问候句型

### 打招呼
| 句型 | 中文 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Hello! | 你好！ | 通用打招呼 |
| Hi! | 嗨！ | 非正式打招呼 |
| Good morning! | 早上好！ | 上午使用 |
| Good afternoon! | 下午好！ | 下午使用 |
| Good evening! | 晚上好！ | 晚上使用 |

### 介绍自己
| 句型 | 中文 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| I'm... / I am... | 我是... | I'm Tom. |
| My name is... | 我叫... | My name is Amy. |
| Nice to meet you. | 很高兴认识你。 | 回答：Nice to meet you, too. |

### 询问姓名
| 句型 | 中文 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| What's your name? | 你叫什么名字？ | I'm Tom. / My name is Tom. |
| How do you do? | 你好！（初次见面） | How do you do? |

## 颜色句型

|------|------|------|
| 红色 | red | It's red. |
| 蓝色 | blue | The sky is blue. |
| 黄色 | yellow | The banana is yellow. |
| 绿色 | green | The grass is green. |
| 黑色 | black | The cat is black. |
| 白色 | white | The snow is white. |
| 橙色 | orange | The orange is orange. |

### 颜色提问
| 句型 | 中文 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| What color is it? | 它是什么颜色？ | It's red. |
| What color do you like? | 你喜欢什么颜色？ | I like blue. |

## 物品句型

### 这是什么
| 句型 | 中文 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| What's this? | 这是什么？ | It's a book. |
| What's that? | 那是什么？ | It's a pen. |
| Is this a...? | 这是一个...吗？ | Yes, it is. / No, it isn't. |

### 物品位置
| 句型 | 中文 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| Where is...? | ...在哪里？ | It's on the desk. |
| Is it in/on/under...? | 它在...里/上/下面吗？ | Yes, it is. / No, it isn't. |

## 人物句型

### 询问是谁
| 句型 | 中文 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| Who is he/she? | 他/她是谁？ | He's my father. |
| Who are they? | 他们是谁？ | They are my friends. |

### 描述人物
| 句型 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| He/She is... | 他/她是... | He is tall. |
| He/She has... | 他/她有... | She has big eyes. |
| He/She likes... | 他/她喜欢... | He likes basketball. |

## 时间句型

### 询问时间
| 句型 | 中文 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| What time is it? | 现在几点？ | It's seven o'clock. |
| When do you...? | 你什么时候...？ | I get up at seven. |

### 时间表达
| 时间 | 英语 | 中文 |
|------|------|------|
| 7:00 | seven o'clock | 七点 |
| 7:30 | seven thirty / half past seven | 七点半 |
| 7:15 | seven fifteen / a quarter past seven | 七点十五 |
| 7:45 | seven forty-five / a quarter to eight | 七点四十五 |

## 日常活动句型

### 能力表达
| 句型 | 中文 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| Can you...? | 你会...吗？ | Yes, I can. / No, I can't. |
| I can... | 我会... | I can swim. |

### 喜好表达
| 句型 | 中文 | 回答 |
|------|------|------|
| Do you like...? | 你喜欢...吗？ | Yes, I do. / No, I don't. |
| What do you like? | 你喜欢什么？ | I like apples. |
| What's your favorite...? | 你最喜欢的...是什么？ | My favorite color is blue. |

### 日常活动
| 句型 | 中文 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| I usually... | 我通常... | I usually get up at seven. |
| I often... | 我经常... | I often play basketball. |
| I sometimes... | 我有时... | I sometimes watch TV. |

## 常用问答

### 简单问答
| 问题 | 回答 |
|------|------|
| How are you? | I'm fine, thank you. / I'm good. |
| How old are you? | I'm ten years old. |
| Where are you from? | I'm from China. |
| What's your job? | I'm a student. |
| How many people are there in your family? | There are four people in my family. |

### 礼貌用语
| 句型 | 中文 | 用法 |
|------|------|------|
| Thank you. | 谢谢你。 | 别人帮助后 |
| You're welcome. | 不客气。 | 回应Thank you |
| Excuse me. | 打扰一下。 | 请求帮助 |
| I'm sorry. | 对不起。 | 表示歉意 |
| That's OK. / That's all right. | 没关系。 | 回应道歉 |
